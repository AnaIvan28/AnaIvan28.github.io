Avoid These Common Gaming Spam Triggers

💰

Money Terms

Avoid: "Free money", "Guaranteed win", "Risk-free". Use: "Bonus offer", "Promotional credit", "Welcome package".

🎰

Casino Phrases

Avoid: "Easy money", "Sure bet", "100% winner". Use: "Gaming experience", "Entertainment value", "Play responsibly".

⚡

Urgency Words

Avoid: "Act now!", "Limited time!", "Hurry!". Use: "Available today", "Current promotion", "This week's offer".

📧

Email Structure

Avoid: ALL CAPS, multiple exclamation marks!!!, $$$. Use: Proper capitalization, professional tone, clear CTAs.

🌍

Compliance Terms

Always include: Age restrictions (18+), responsible gaming links, unsubscribe options, license information.

🔒

Trust Signals

Include: License numbers, security badges, customer support info, clear terms and conditions.
